What is HackNC?

HackNC is the South's most memorable hackathon for college students and aspiring programmers and engineers. At HackNC you'll learn new things, make cool stuff, eat great food, meet new friends. In the fall, the UNC Computer Science Department hosts a large hackathon for software and hardware. In the spring, we host fun day-long events that focus on a specific topic. Enter, Raspberry Jam.

What is a Raspberry Jam?

One platform for hardware hacks is the Raspberry Pi - a simple microcontroller capable of running Linux and controlling hardware perhipherals. The Raspberry Pi Foundation sponsors events called Raspberry Jams for enthusiasts to share knowledge, learn new things, and meet other builders. The Raspberry Jam at UNC is about all kinds of hardware, but since we got our name from the Raspberry Pi Foundation, we'll have lots of Raspberry Pis to build on too. Want to play with an Arduino? An IC? Even a ...BeagleBoard? We'll have enough hardware for everyone.
